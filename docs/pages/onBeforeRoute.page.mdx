The `onBeforeRoute()` hook gives us full control over the routing process.

For example, the hook can be used to [internationalize a pre-rendered app](/i18n#with-pre-rendering) or to [implement a custom router](https://github.com/brillout/vite-plugin-ssr/issues/140).
